About the Property

Welcome to our 100m² private apartment occupying half of a charming stone villa in Gattières village. This beautiful property sleeps 4 guests comfortably and offers a perfect blend of privacy, comfort, and authentic French Riviera living.

Just 3 minutes from Gattières village center and 20 minutes from Nice and the beach, you'll enjoy easy access to both peaceful countryside and vibrant coastal attractions. The property features a private entrance, 2,500m² of private gardens, and a heated pool available from April to October.

About Your Hosts

Your hosts, Liz and Quentin Murphy, are a retired couple with over 25 years of rental experience. Quentin is an ex-captain, while Liz is a former air hostess and talented artist whose artwork is displayed throughout the property.

Consistently rated 10/10 by repeat guests, they take pride in ensuring every stay is comfortable and memorable. Their warm hospitality and attention to detail have made Villa Le Contadour a favorite among travelers seeking an authentic French Riviera experience.

Description

Located in the picturesque village of Gattières, this villa offers the perfect balance between authentic Provençal charm and modern comfort. Surrounded by narrow streets, stone houses, and breathtaking views over the Var valley, the village is a hidden gem just a few minutes from the coast.

The property itself boasts 100m² of living space with two refined bedrooms, a spacious living room, and a fully equipped open kitchen. Large windows fill the rooms with natural light, while the tasteful décor combines elegance and warmth.

Outside, guests can enjoy a private heated pool and a terrace with panoramic views of the surrounding hills and villages. The private garden is ideal for relaxing afternoons, family meals, or a quiet evening under the stars.

Just a 15-minute drive away, you will find the beaches of Saint-Laurent-du-Var and the vibrant city of Nice, with its famous Promenade des Anglais, restaurants, and cultural attractions. The artistic village of Saint-Paul-de-Vence is also nearby, perfect for exploring galleries and local history.

Whether you enjoy hiking in the surrounding mountains, strolling through charming medieval streets, or discovering the lively atmosphere of the French Riviera, this villa offers an ideal starting point for a memorable holiday.
